If you're facing an emergency or need immediate help, don't hesitate to contact the respective helplines.

  • National Police Helpline: 112
  • Women Helpline: 181
  • Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

What is Cyber Crime?

Cyber crime refers to any criminal activity that involves a computer, network, or device. These crimes can take many forms, including hacking, identity theft, online fraud, and harassment. The National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal helps you to report such crimes and ensures the safety of digital spaces.
